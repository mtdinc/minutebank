Minute Bank

A gamified study timer for students who want to earn guilt-free fun time.

About the App

Minute Bank helps students overcome procrastination through gamified accountability:

  • Study — Complete focused study sessions (25+ minutes)
  • Earn — Get "minute coins" based on your study time
  • Spend — Unlock rewards like shows, games, or social media
  • Focus — Optionally block distracting apps during study

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 4, 2026

Data We Collect

  • Account info: Email address (for login via Supabase Auth)
  • Study data: Session timestamps, durations, tags, and earned rewards
  • App preferences: Settings, goals, and customization choices

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Which specific apps you block or use
  • Screen Time or usage statistics
  • Screenshots or screen recordings
  • Location data

Screen Time API

Minute Bank uses Apple's Screen Time API to let you block distracting apps during study sessions. This feature is entirely optional and self-directed. All app blocking data stays on your device — we cannot see which apps you block or how long you use them.

Data Storage

Your study data syncs to Supabase (cloud database) so you can access it across devices. Your blocked app selections stay on-device only.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We do not display ads.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

Minute Bank is designed for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.
