About The Vault (Screen Time API): We use Apple's FamilyControls framework to let you block apps. Your app selections are stored as opaque tokens ‚Äî we literally cannot see which apps you chose. Usage time is tracked locally on your device to enforce time limits, then discarded. This data never leaves your device or reaches our servers.
About Hardcore Mode (Apple Watch): If you enable Hardcore Mode, the Watch uses motion sensors (CMPedometer) to detect if you walk away during a session. Step counts are processed in real-time to verify presence and are never stored or transmitted. This feature requires your explicit permission.

Social Features (Optional)

If you use social features, you can set a public username and add friends. Your username and study stats appear on leaderboards. Friends can see your activity feed (sessions completed, levels gained). You control who you add as friends.

Social features are entirely optional. The app works fully without them.

Data Storage & Security

Backend: Your data syncs to Supabase, a secure cloud database with row-level security. Each user can only access their own data.

Local: Device data (Vault selections, widget state) uses Apple's secure UserDefaults and App Groups.

No tracking: We do not use third-party analytics, crash reporters, or advertising SDKs.

Why We Process Your Data (Legal Basis)

Contract performance: We process your account and game data to provide the app's core services.

Legitimate interest: We may analyze aggregated, anonymized usage patterns to improve the app.

Consent: Social features and motion tracking require your explicit opt-in.

Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Access: Export your study history and game data from the app.

Correction: Update your profile information anytime.

Deletion: Delete your account and all cloud data via Settings.

Portability: Request a copy of your data in a standard format.

No sale of data: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Ever.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data is removed from our servers within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated statistics may be retained for analytics.

Children's Privacy

Minute Bank is designed for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
